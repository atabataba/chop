about

  1. mission
    An emerging self-sustaining community centered on Zen practice, simple living, and harmony with the natural world.
  2. teachings
    Practice is grounded in the teachings of Suzuki Roshi and Dōgen Zenji, integrating meditation, study, and physical work in farm and craft as one continuous expression of Zen.
  3. community
    A new practice center is taking shape in Hawai‘i, calling for people to help build its foundations and form a Zen community.
  4. residency
    Zazen, work, and study form the core of daily life and practice here.

The residency schedule is organized in ten-day cycles based on the lunar calendar. Days ending in zero are rest days, and days ending in five are half days.

Ten-Day Cycle

  • Days 1–4: Follow the daily schedule above.
  • Day 5: Half-day — schedule through lunch, then free time.
  • Days 6–9: Follow the daily schedule above.
  • Day 10: Breakfast, morning chores, and rest for the day.
